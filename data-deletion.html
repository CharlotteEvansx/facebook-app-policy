User Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: May 9, 2026

This page explains how users can request deletion of personal data associated with RareFoot, our website, application, tools, and services connected with https://rarefoot.com.

1. How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your personal data, please send an email to:

support@rarefoot.com

Please use the subject line:

Data Deletion Request

2. Information to Include

To help us identify your account and process your request, please include the following information in your email:

  • Your full name, if available.
  • The email address associated with your RareFoot account.
  • The Facebook, Instagram, or Meta account identifier connected to our app, if applicable.
  • A short statement confirming that you want your data deleted.

3. Meta / Facebook Connected Data

If you connected our app through Facebook, Instagram, or another Meta platform, we may have received limited information authorized by you, such as your public profile, email address, account ID, Page information, business asset information, or related metadata.

Upon receiving and verifying your deletion request, we will delete or anonymize the personal data associated with your connected Meta account, unless we are required to retain certain information for legal, security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or compliance purposes.

4. Disconnecting the App from Meta

You may also remove or revoke our app’s access through your Facebook, Instagram, or Meta account settings. Removing app access from Meta may stop future data access, but it may not automatically delete data already stored by us. To request deletion of stored data, please email us using the instructions above.

5. Processing Time

We will review and process valid data deletion requests within a reasonable period of time after verifying the request. We may contact you if additional information is needed to verify your identity or locate your account.

6. Data That May Be Retained

Some information may be retained where necessary for legal compliance, security, fraud prevention, accounting, dispute resolution, enforcement of agreements, or legitimate business recordkeeping. Where retention is required, we will limit the retained information to what is necessary.

7. Contact

For data deletion requests or privacy-related questions, contact us at:

RareFoot
Website: https://rarefoot.com
Email: support@rarefoot.com
